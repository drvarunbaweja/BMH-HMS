Privacy Policy

This trial page explains the intended patient data flow before production launch.

Appointment details shared through this review website are for testing only. On GitHub Pages, trial form submissions are stored locally in the reviewer's browser and are not sent to hospital staff, ad platforms, or a database.

Before public launch, lead forms should be connected to a secure approved destination such as a CRM, encrypted database, or controlled hospital email workflow. Advertising pixels should receive conversion events, not sensitive medical concerns.
