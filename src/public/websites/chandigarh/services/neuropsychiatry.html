REGION FIRST

Biofeedback Therapy — First in the Region

BMH was the first hospital in the Chandigarh Tricity region to introduce biofeedback therapy — a clinically validated, drug-free treatment for anxiety, migraines, hypertension, and stress-related conditions.

What is Biofeedback?

Biofeedback is a mind-body technique that uses sensors placed on your body to measure physiological signals — heart rate, muscle tension, skin temperature, and brainwave activity. These signals are displayed in real time on a screen, allowing you to see what's happening inside your own body.

With a trained therapist's guidance, you learn to consciously control these responses — reducing anxiety, managing migraines, lowering blood pressure, and improving sleep. No drugs, no side effects, no dependence.

DE-ADDICTION PROGRAMME

Recovery is Possible. We're With You.

Our structured, confidential de-addiction programme covers alcohol, drug, and tobacco dependency — combining medical stabilisation with psychological support for lasting recovery.

Alcohol De-addiction
Safe medical detoxification, withdrawal management, and relapse-prevention counselling. We address the physical, psychological, and social dimensions of alcohol dependency.
Drug De-addiction
Evidence-based opioid substitution therapy, benzodiazepine tapering, and stimulant dependency treatment under close medical supervision in a non-judgmental setting.
Tobacco Cessation
Structured nicotine replacement, medication-assisted cessation, and behavioural therapy — proven to significantly improve success rates compared to willpower alone.
Medical + Psychological Integration
Addiction is not a moral failing — it is a medical condition. Our programme combines pharmacotherapy with individual counselling, family support, and relapse prevention planning.
Complete Confidentiality

Aftercare & Family Support

An EEG (Electroencephalogram) is a painless test that records electrical activity in the brain using small sensors placed on the scalp. It is used to investigate and monitor epilepsy, sleep disorders, encephalopathy, and certain psychiatric conditions. The test takes approximately 30–45 minutes and is performed by our trained technicians under medical supervision.
