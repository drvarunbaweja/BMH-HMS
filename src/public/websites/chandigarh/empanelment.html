Payments

Transparent Billing Before Treatment

Clear estimates, private insurance paperwork support, and factual guidance without claiming government scheme empanelment.

Written Estimate

Patients receive a clear estimate before planned procedures wherever possible, including likely inclusions and expected follow-up needs.

Insurance Documents

Our team can help prepare clinical papers, invoices and discharge summaries for private insurance or reimbursement where the patient's own policy allows it.

No False Empanelment Claims

BMH Chandigarh is not currently listed as CGHS, ECHS, PM-JAY, ESI or NABH empanelled. This page keeps that clear for patient trust.

What to Bring

  • Previous medical records and investigation reports.
  • Insurance card or policy details, if applicable.
  • Any referral note, approval letter or reimbursement form provided by the insurer.
