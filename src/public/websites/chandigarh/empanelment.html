Your Insurance Works Here

Empaneled with CGHS, ECHS, Ayushman Bharat and all major insurance providers — cashless treatment across all departments.

CGHS ECHS Ayushman Bharat ESI All Major TPAs

Baweja Multispeciality Hospital is empaneled with India's leading government health schemes and private insurance providers. We work directly with your insurer to ensure cashless, hassle-free treatment so you can focus entirely on recovery — not paperwork. Our dedicated billing team handles pre-authorisation, claim documentation, and insurer coordination from admission to discharge.

GOVERNMENT SCHEMES

Central & State Health Schemes

If you are a government employee, pensioner, armed forces veteran, or beneficiary of Ayushman Bharat — your treatment at BMH is fully covered.

CGHS
Central Government Health Scheme
For Central Govt. Employees & Pensioners

CGHS covers central government employees, pensioners, and their dependants for OPD consultations, in-patient treatment, surgical procedures, and diagnostic investigations. All specialities at BMH are covered under the CGHS rate list.

How to Avail
Present your CGHS card at registration. For OPD, no prior authorisation required. For planned surgeries and hospitalisation, our billing team will coordinate pre-authorisation directly with the CGHS office.
ECHS
Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme
For Retired Armed Forces Personnel & Families

ECHS covers retired Indian armed forces personnel and their families for comprehensive medical care including specialist consultations, major surgeries, diagnostic services, and hospitalisation at ECHS empaneled hospitals like BMH.

How to Avail
Present your ECHS card and referral slip from your polyclinic (if required) at registration. Full coverage for major procedures. Our team handles all documentation and claim submission on your behalf.
AB
Ayushman Bharat – PM-JAY
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY provides cashless treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for economically weaker sections. Coverage includes surgical procedures, hospitalisation, medicines, diagnostics, and post-discharge follow-up. Over 1,900 medical packages are covered.

How to Avail
Bring your Ayushman Bharat card (or Aadhaar with eligible beneficiary ID). Our team will verify eligibility on the PM-JAY portal, complete pre-authorisation, and ensure fully cashless treatment throughout your stay.
ESI
Employee State Insurance
For Organised Sector Employees

ESI covers employees in the organised sector earning up to the ESI wage ceiling for comprehensive medical benefits including OPD treatment, hospitalisation, specialist consultations, surgery, and maternity benefits for insured women.

How to Avail
Present your ESI card and employer ESI registration details at registration. ESI referral from your dispensary may be required for specialist services. Our billing team will guide you through the process.

SIMPLE PROCESS

How Cashless Treatment Works at BMH

Four straightforward steps — we handle the insurance paperwork so you can focus on getting better.

1

Inform at Registration

When you arrive at BMH, inform our registration desk of your insurance provider, policy number, and TPA. Present your insurance card and a photo ID.

2

Pre-Authorisation

Our dedicated billing team contacts your insurer or TPA to submit a pre-authorisation request with your diagnosis, proposed treatment plan, and estimated costs.

3

Treatment Proceeds

Once authorisation is received, your treatment proceeds on a cashless basis. We manage all insurer communication and documentation throughout your care.

4

Direct Insurer Settlement

At discharge, your insurer pays the approved amount directly to BMH. You pay only non-covered expenses, co-pays, or exclusions (if any) — nothing more.

REIMBURSEMENT CLAIMS

Insurer Not on Our Direct List?

If your insurance company is not currently on our direct billing panel, you can still receive full treatment at BMH and claim reimbursement from your insurer. We provide a complete, well-organised documentation package to support your claim.

Our billing team will prepare your claim file so it meets your insurer's requirements — reducing the likelihood of queries or delays in reimbursement. Most reimbursement claims are processed within 15–30 days by insurers.
