MINIMALLY INVASIVE · GLAUCOMA SURGERY

The MIGS Revolution
Starts in Chandigarh

MIGS — Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery — represents a paradigm shift in glaucoma treatment. Dr. Varun Baweja brings NHS-trained surgical precision to deliver micro-incision procedures that drastically reduce intraocular pressure while minimising recovery time.

  • ✦ Micro-incision — minimal surgical trauma
  • ✦ Faster recovery vs traditional surgery
  • ✦ Suitable for mild to moderate glaucoma
  • ✦ Often combined with cataract surgery
  • ✦ Reduces or eliminates need for eye drops
