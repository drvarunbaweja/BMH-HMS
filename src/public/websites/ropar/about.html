Our Story

A Surgeon's Promise to Punjab

Dr. Varun Baweja grew up in Punjab, watching patients travel hundreds of kilometres to cities like Chandigarh or Delhi for specialist eye care. He knew that world-class surgery should be accessible to every family — not just those who could afford to travel.

After completing his MBBS and MS in Ophthalmology, Dr. Varun pursued an Advanced Fellowship at Moorfields Eye Hospital NHS in London — the world's most prestigious centre for eye care. There, he trained alongside global leaders in cataract surgery, glaucoma, cornea, and MIGS (Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery).

The MIGS Revolution, Brought to Punjab

Upon returning to India, Dr. Varun Baweja made history — becoming the First surgeon in India to perform the iStent Inject W procedure, and the First in the region to introduce the Hydrus Microstent. These pioneering procedures offer glaucoma patients a safer, faster recovery compared to traditional surgery.

Today, Baweja Hospital Ropar is equipped with the same class of diagnostic and surgical technology used at leading NHS centres — including OCT imaging, corneal topography, phacoemulsification, SMILE Pro laser vision correction, and the latest MIGS instrumentation.

Rooted in Ropar, Serving Punjab

We serve patients from Ropar, Rupnagar, Morinda, Anandpur Sahib, Nangal, Fatehgarh Sahib, and Chamkaur Sahib. Our mission is simple: bring NHS-quality care to the people of Punjab — at accessible, transparent prices, with genuine compassion.
