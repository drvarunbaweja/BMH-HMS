Safe Delivery

Your Safe Delivery — Our Priority

Every birth is unique. Dr. Namrata's philosophy is to support the mother's birth plan, while always prioritising the safety of mother and baby. We encourage normal delivery wherever medically appropriate — and are equipped for all obstetric emergencies.

High-Risk Conditions We Manage
  • Gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM)
  • Pregnancy-induced hypertension / Pre-eclampsia
  • Multiple pregnancy (twins / triplets)
  • VBAC (vaginal birth after caesarean)
  • Advanced maternal age (35+)
  • Placenta praevia / abruption
  • Foetal growth restriction (IUGR)
  • Previous pregnancy loss / recurrent miscarriage
Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally Invasive Gynaecological Surgery

Smaller incisions. Less pain. Faster recovery. Back home in 24–48 hours for most laparoscopic procedures.

🔵
Ovarian Cyst Removal

Laparoscopic cystectomy preserving ovarian function wherever possible. Same-day discharge for most cases.

🟢
Laparoscopic Hysterectomy

Removal of the uterus via keyhole surgery — significantly less pain and faster recovery compared to open surgery.

🟡
Fibroid Removal

Laparoscopic myomectomy to remove fibroids while preserving the uterus — important for women still wanting to conceive.

🔴
Tubal Ligation

Permanent contraception by laparoscopic tubal occlusion — a 20-minute day-care procedure with rapid return to daily activities.

🟣
Diagnostic Laparoscopy

Pelvic pain investigation, endometriosis diagnosis and treatment, adhesiolysis — when imaging alone is insufficient.

🩷
Hysteroscopy

Investigation and treatment of intrauterine pathology — polyps, fibroids, septum, abnormal bleeding — via the natural opening.
