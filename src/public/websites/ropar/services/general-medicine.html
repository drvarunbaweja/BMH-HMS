Diabetes in Punjab

Chronic Disease Management

Diabetes — Punjab's Silent Epidemic

Punjab has one of India's highest diabetes prevalence rates — driven by dietary habits, lifestyle changes, and genetic factors. Uncontrolled diabetes causes serious complications including diabetic retinopathy (eye damage), kidney disease, neuropathy, and heart disease.

At Baweja Hospital, we take a holistic approach to diabetes management — combining medical treatment with dietary counselling, lifestyle coaching, and complication screening. Uniquely, we can also screen for diabetic retinopathy in the same visit with Dr. Varun Baweja's eye clinic.

Our Diabetes Care Protocol

  • →Fasting and post-prandial blood glucose monitoring
  • →HbA1c assessment every 3 months
  • →Personalised diet and exercise plan
  • →Medication review and optimisation
  • →Annual retinal screening (Dr. Varun Baweja — eye clinic)
  • →Kidney function and urine ACR monitoring
  • →Foot care education and peripheral neuropathy assessment

Early Detection Saves Lives

Many of Punjab's most common health problems — diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders — have no early symptoms. By the time most patients present to a doctor, organ damage has already begun.

Our preventive health checks are designed to detect these conditions early — before complications arise. A single visit to our OPD can provide a comprehensive picture of your health and guide personalised prevention.
